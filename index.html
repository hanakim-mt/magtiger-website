Frequently Asked

In Plain Words.

What is MagTiger?
MagTiger is Asia's beauty venture studio — a strategic orchestrator headquartered in Songdo, Korea. We help excellent Asian beauty, CPG, and packaging companies become investment-grade and reach their next valuation moment — exit, IPO, strategic acquisition, or scaling event.
How is MagTiger different from a regular ODM agent or beauty consultancy?
ODM agents broker manufacturing capacity. Consultancies write reports. MagTiger owns outcomes. We embed as operating partner with NY Bar legal credentials, 15+ years of beauty industry execution, and direct relationships with PE funds and strategic acquirers across Asia, Europe, and North America.
How is this different from an investment bank?
Investment banks intermediate transactions that already exist. MagTiger builds the company that becomes the transaction — operationally and narratively, 18–36 months before the bank shows up.
Why beauty? Why Asia? Why now?
Beauty in Asia is at peak institutionalization. KKR's acquisition of Samhwa (₩733B, 2025), L'Oréal's acquisition of Dr.G (2025), K-Beauty US export +53% YoY (1Q25). 2026 is mid-cycle entry. By 2027 prime asset shortage begins. The window is 12–18 months.
Are you only beauty?
Beauty, CPG, and packaging are flagship verticals. MagTiger extends selectively to adjacent FMCG categories — fashion, food, personal care, wellness — when the network and operating logic transfer cleanly. SEA brand owners and Korean specialty manufacturers in the MagTiger network span beyond pure beauty. Engagements outside the network depth are declined.
What does a typical engagement look like?
Three modes. Growth Partnership (12–36 month embedded operating role for revenue scaling). Capability Embedding (region-specific advisory layer for PE-backed platforms). Transaction Execution (M&A advisory, exit prep, PMI). Each engagement starts with a 30-day diagnostic and a written outcome thesis.
What's MagTiger's fee structure?
Monthly retainer + performance commission tied to outcomes (PO value, deal value, milestone delivery) + reimbursable expenses at cost. Below-market base rate signals long-term partnership intent. PE-grade transparency — every commission base disclosed in writing before engagement starts.
How does MagTiger measure success?
Every engagement has a written outcome thesis. KPIs are agreed within 60 days of start. Four measurement areas: sourcing throughput, active project pipeline, cycle time reduction, and strategic milestone delivery. Annual review with explicit fee, scope, and KPI reset.
Who does MagTiger NOT work with?
Companies with no path to a valuation moment. Founders unwilling to take operating advice. Engagements requiring exclusivity outside MagTiger's regional or category strength. Selection discipline is the basis of execution quality.
What's MagTiger's investment thesis?
Excellent Asian companies fail to reach institutional capital not because of product quality but because of three structural gaps — operational expertise to scale, direct access to capital, and credible storytelling. Asian beauty category leaders often have all the substance but none of the narrative infrastructure. MagTiger fills that gap. Every engagement is structured around four pillars: operational expertise, access to capital, network, storytelling.

Anonymized — an SEA ODM, Japanese PE portfolio company. Cross-border PMI strategy, KPI restructuring, ODM exports 3× in 33 months, strategic exit to Japanese acquirer. Concrete numbers, named acquirer category — disclosure level is what counts in PE-grade work.
