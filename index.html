Frequently Asked

In Plain Words.

What is MagTiger?
MagTiger is a strategic orchestration company based in Songdo International City, Korea. It connects Korean, Japanese, Southeast Asian, and Chinese beauty manufacturers with global brands and capital. MagTiger is not a factory and not an agency — it monetizes 15 years of trust-built networks to make cross-border beauty deals happen.
What does MagTiger do differently from a regular ODM factory or beauty agency?
An ODM factory sells production volume and capacity through a vendor-to-client relationship. A Western beauty agency sells brand design and development as a service provider. MagTiger is a strategic orchestrator: it operates partner-to-partner, leveraging a live personal network across 9+ Asian countries and offering full-spectrum advisory including M&A, exit strategy, OBM transition, and channel architecture.
What is K-Beauty ODM orchestration?
K-Beauty ODM orchestration is the end-to-end coordination of cross-border beauty product development — matching a global brand's brief with the specific Korean, Japanese, or Southeast Asian manufacturer that has the right formula, capacity, and regulatory fit. MagTiger collapses a typical 6-month sourcing cycle into weeks by pre-screening partners, negotiating terms, and managing delivery.
Who founded MagTiger?
MagTiger was founded by Ha Na Kim, a New York State Bar licensed attorney with 15 years of operational execution in the beauty industry. Her track record includes growing an ODM subsidiary from $5M to $22M in 3 years (+440%), 3× ODM export growth in 33 months at a Japanese PE portfolio company, and leading the L'Oréal Paris and Maybelline cushion program of 100M+ units at Cosmax Korea.
Where is MagTiger located?
MagTiger is headquartered at 26FL, POSCO Tower-Songdo, in Songdo International City, Korea. The company operates an active network across Korea, Japan, Thailand, China, Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines, Vietnam, Cambodia, Myanmar, Australia, and North America.
What industries and clients does MagTiger serve?
MagTiger serves global retailers, brand houses, FMCG companies, K-Beauty and J-Beauty manufacturers, Southeast Asian beauty brand owners, private equity investors, and exporters. Engagement structures are flexible — advisory, transaction, or project-based — tailored to each partnership.
What languages does MagTiger operate in?
MagTiger's team covers seven languages natively or professionally: Korean, English, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Thai, Malay/Bahasa, and Arabic. This enables real-time cross-border briefs without translation delay.
